# **AK's Guide to Fluffy Companions**
## *Volume IV: Much Ado About (Rough) Collies*

![majestic blue](https://live.staticflickr.com/3610/3292118680_81bee1178d_b.jpg)

Welcome to Volume IV of *AK's Guide to Fluffy Companions*! In this volume, we will meet the wonderful rough collie, beloved througout the world. Please note: **not to be mistaken for large shelties or fluffy corgis.**

## *"They're so cuddly!"*
![cuddly collies](https://i.pinimg.com/originals/ae/e3/be/aee3be8896838b806250c160e6d0d6a8.jpg)
They may be prone to stealing your favorite spot on the couch, but I choose to believe that they do so out of love. Besides, so long as you refrain from upsetting them, they tend to make wonderful pillows.

### Fluffy *and* friendly!?
It's true - these pups have a good reputation for their kind nature!
